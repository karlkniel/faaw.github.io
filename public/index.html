ABOUT FAAW

The furniture industry is in need of healing. If you've found yourself uttering the phrase, 'They dont make 'em like they used to,' a little too often recently, you've come to the right place. At First Aid Architectural Woodworking we believe that furniture should not only look great but should also act as a tool to support your lifestyle. We believe that a quality piece should never have to be thrown away. Our furniture is efficiently designed for people who value form and function equally. Our work is built using quality materials and hardware that not only stand the test of time but can be easily repaired after years of use.


They don’t make ‘em like First Aid.
