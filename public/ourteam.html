THE TEAM



DESIGNER : EMILY SPEAS

Emily graduated with a Bachelor of Design from the University of Florida in 2017 and recently completed her Masters of Architecture at UF in 2020. She finds inspiration through travel and spent four months abroad studying at the Vicenza Institute of Architecture. In 2019, Emily completed a year-long internship with Walt Disney Imagineering, which fueled her creativity and passion for design. Her simple, clean, and efficient architectural style translates effortlessly to smaller scale, handmade furniture pieces.


















CARPENTER : KARL KNIEL

Karl is a native Floridian currently working as a Software Engineer in Denver, Colorado. He graduated from the University of Florida in 2018 with a degree in Computer Engineering and a minor in Physics. His technical skills stem from his grandfathers, one a chemical engineer and the other a home builder in Central Florida in the 60s. Karl has cultivated his carpentry skills for the last 6 years producing multiple pieces for family and friends. He has a great love for the outdoors and finds inspiration in nature.
